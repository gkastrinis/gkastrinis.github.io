George Kastrinis

Hey! I'm George. I hold a PhD from the University of Athens, Greece, where I am currently working as a researcher with Prof. Yannis Smaragdakis on our programming languages and software technologies group (PLaST). My research interests include Programming Languages and Static Pointer Analysis, using Datalog and the declarative Doop framework.

My latest project is the paNda library. The aim is to provide a programmable compiler for Datalog.
